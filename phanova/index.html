Get Started For Physicians

How It Works

Three simple steps to better care between appointments.

Step 1

We Check In

You'll receive a friendly text or call from our system asking simple questions: How's your blood pressure today? Did you take your medication? How are you feeling?

Step 2

You Respond

Reply with a simple text or tell us over the phone. It takes seconds. No apps to install, no complicated forms to fill out—just you and your phone.

Step 3

Your Doctor Knows

Your responses are instantly organized into a report for your doctor. If something looks off, they know immediately—not three months from now at your next appointment.
