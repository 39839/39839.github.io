About Phanova

Healthcare That Doesn't Stop at the Clinic Door

Phanova was built on a simple observation: The current system of "see you in three months" wasn't working for chronic care. Doctors wanted to help their patients more. Patients wanted to stay healthy. But the system made it nearly impossible to stay connected between appointments.

We founded Phanova to bridge that gap. Not by replacing doctors—but by making doctors more accessible. We use technology to extend your physician's reach without burning out their staff, and we give you the daily support you need to manage your condition successfully.

Our Approach

Built for both patients and physicians—because great care requires both.

For Patients

Simple by Design

We know you're dealing with enough already. That's why Phanova is designed to be simple. No apps to download. No logins to remember. Just a text or a call, a quick response, and the peace of mind that your doctor is staying informed about your health.

For Physicians

Built to Scale

We know you're stretched thin. That's why Phanova automates the routine parts of chronic care management—the daily check-ins, the medication reminders, the data collection—so your team can focus on the clinical decisions that actually require your expertise.
