About Phanova

Empowering providers to engage patients easily, efficiently, and securely.

A healthtech startup building AI-powered communication solutions that help clinics expand chronic care programs without increasing staff labor.

Our mission

Transform patient engagement for chronic care management through intelligent automation.

Patient First

Seamless engagement

We believe patients deserve consistent, personalized touchpoints without overwhelming clinical teams. Our AI agents handle routine outreach so providers can focus on care.

Provider Focused

Scale without staffing

Chronic care programs shouldn't require endless hiring. We automate patient communication, care plan generation, and billing tracking to expand enrollment efficiently.

Built for Healthcare

Compliant by design

Healthcare demands security and compliance. Every conversation is HIPAA-aligned, every interaction is documented, and every touchpoint maintains the trust patients expect.

What we do

AI-powered solutions that automate the entire CCM and RPM workflow.

Outreach

Text and voice AI agents

Automated patient communication via text messages and phone calls that collect vital signs, medication adherence, and symptom updates at your defined cadence.

  • Natural conversations patients actually respond to.
  • No apps required—works via text and voice.
  • Customized to your clinic's protocols.
Care Plans

Automatic care plan generation

Individualized care plans created and updated automatically for every patient in your CCM program, reflecting your clinic's standards and each patient's unique needs.

  • Generated from your protocols and preferences.
  • Updated as patient data changes.
  • Shared securely with patients.
Billing

Time tracking and reporting

Complete tracking of all patient interactions and time spent, with monthly billing reports ready for CCM code submission and audit-ready documentation.

  • Automatic calculation of time per patient.
  • Monthly reports for CCM/RPM billing.
  • Complete audit trails for compliance.
