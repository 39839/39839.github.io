Turn episodic care into continuous monitoring. Phanova automates patient outreach, improves adherence, and simplifies CCM billing—with zero added burden on your staff.

The Problem You Face

You know your chronic care patients need closer monitoring. You want to check in on them, ensure they're taking their medications, catch problems before they become emergencies. But you don't have the staff hours to call dozens of patients every day.

So patients drift between appointments. They forget medications. Their blood pressure creeps up. And you don't find out until they're back in your office three months later—or worse, in the ER.

The Phanova Solution

Phanova acts as an automated extension of your clinical staff. Our system handles the routine outreach via text and voice calls, collecting the data you need to make clinical decisions. Think of it as having a dedicated care coordinator for every single CCM patient—without adding headcount.

Key Features for Providers

Everything you need to scale your chronic care management program.

Dashboard

Real-Time Statistics Hub

Stop guessing how your patients are doing. Our dashboard gives you a live view of patient adherence and vitals. You can set specific goals for each patient (target blood pressure, daily steps, medication compliance) and track their progress visually. See at a glance who's doing well and who needs attention.

Outreach

Automated Daily Outreach

The system reaches out to your patients daily, weekly, or at whatever cadence you define. It asks about vitals, medication adherence, and symptoms. Patients respond via text or voice—no app installation required on their end.

Care Plans

Individualized Care Plans

We automatically generate personalized care plans tailored to each patient's conditions and your clinic's protocols. These aren't generic templates—they reflect your practice's approach to care. And they update automatically as patient data changes.

Setup

Seamless Onboarding

No complex EHR integration required. We can import your CCM candidates from a simple spreadsheet and get them started immediately. Most clinics are up and running within a week.

Billing

Billing Made Simple

We track every interaction—every text message, every phone call, every minute spent. At the end of the month, you get a clean billing report showing exactly how much time was spent on each patient, ready for you to claim your CCM reimbursement codes. No more guessing or manual time tracking.
