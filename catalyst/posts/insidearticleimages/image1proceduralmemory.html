Declarative Memory

Involves the conscious recall of information, such as facts and personally experienced events. Think of it as "knowing that..."

Examples:
  • Remembering a historical date
  • Recalling details of your last vacation
  • Learning new vocabulary words
  • Knowing the capital of a country
Key Brain Areas: Medial Temporal Lobe (e.g., Hippocampus)

Procedural Memory

Supports the non-conscious learning and retrieval of skills and habits. This is your "knowing how..."

Examples:
  • Riding a bicycle effortlessly
  • Typing on a keyboard without looking
  • Playing a familiar song on an instrument
  • Applying grammatical rules automatically
Key Brain Areas: Basal Ganglia, Cerebellum
