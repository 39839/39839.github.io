About The Catalyst

Catalyst (noun): An agent that sparks significant change

Our Mission

At The Catalyst, we strive to live up to our name — serving as a Washington D.C. based student-run magazine that empowers the voices of tomorrow's scientists, innovators, and critical thinkers. Through engaging content rooted in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), we aim to inform, inspire, and spark meaningful change emanating outwards from the Capital of the United States.

Student-Led D.C. Based STEM Focused

What We Do

We showcase the ideas, innovators, and advocates redefining what science can do for society. Our content includes in-depth profiles of researchers, features on breakthrough discoveries, interviews with leading scientists, and thought-provoking editorials on science and policy.

Profiles Features Interviews Editorials

Our Values

We are committed to delivering accurate, objective, and insightful content. Our editorial process emphasizes rigorous fact-checking and peer review, ensuring reliability and credibility. We draw on reputable academic sources and conduct expert interviews to provide well-rounded perspectives.

Accuracy Integrity Excellence

Our Story

From a simple idea to a thriving platform

The Catalyst emerged from a simple idea shared by a handful of students who believed in the transformative power of STEM. Like many grassroots projects, our initial stages were defined by limited funding and juggling tight academic schedules. Despite those challenges, our enthusiasm and teamwork helped turn a budding concept into the thriving platform we have today.

Over time, more contributors joined our efforts, sharing a commitment to highlight the possibilities within STEM and to cultivate a welcoming environment for students at every level of their journey. We remain grateful for the encouragement of our readers, whose feedback and support drive us to continually improve and grow.

As we look toward the future, we remain dedicated to exploring new ideas, sharing inspiring stories, and fostering collaborations that unite STEM students across the D.C. area and beyond. We invite you to explore our content, join the discussion, and be part of a community devoted to igniting change — one idea at a time.

Our Commitment

Standards we uphold in every piece we publish

Fact-Checked Content

Every article goes through our rigorous editorial process. We verify claims with primary sources, consult experts, and ensure accuracy before publication.

Expert Interviews

We conduct in-depth interviews with researchers, scientists, and innovators to bring you first-hand perspectives on groundbreaking work.

Transparency

We are open about our processes, corrections, and editorial decisions. Trust is built through accountability and honest communication.
